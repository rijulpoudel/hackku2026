# LAUNCH — Life After Graduation

> A full-screen narrative financial life simulator for recent college graduates.
> Built for the **Security Benefit** track at **HackKU 2026**.

---

## What Is LAUNCH?

You just graduated. You have **$2,100 in savings** and **$34,000 in student loans**. Now what?

LAUNCH puts you through **12 years of real financial decisions** — ages 22 to 33 — one full-screen scenario at a time. Every choice compounds. Every mistake has a cost. Every smart move builds your future.

---

## The Game

- Start at age 22 with -$31,900 net worth
- Make one major financial decision per year for 12 years
- Watch your net worth change in real time after every choice
- Receive a personalized AI-generated financial verdict at the end

**Decisions cover:**
- Career choices — salary negotiation, job offers, promotions, city vs. hometown
- Lifestyle — rent vs. buy, car choices, relationships, children
- Debt management — student loans, credit cards, mortgages, refinancing
- Savings & investing — emergency fund, 401k, index funds, Roth IRA
- Life events — job loss, medical emergency, inheritance, windfalls
- Tax events — W-4 withholding, filing, deductions, 1099 income

**No obvious right answers.** Every choice has real trade-offs.

## Key Features

### AI-Personalized Scenarios
Years 1–3 use carefully crafted anchor decisions (W-4 withholding, first tax return, 401k enrollment). Years 4–12 are dynamically generated by Gemini based on your unique financial history. Deep in debt? You get debt scenarios. On a roll? You get opportunity scenarios.

### Real Financial Education
Every decision surface a real financial concept — defined in plain English, with a real statistic shown after you choose. No jargon. No lectures. Just consequences.

### Compounding Choices
Decisions you make early affect what scenarios appear later. Skip the 401k in Year 3? You'll feel it in Year 9. Build an emergency fund? That unlocks investment opportunities.

### Custom Character + Voice Cloning
Don't want to play as Maya, Alex, Jordan, or Sam? Build your own. The custom character flow lets you set your own name, pick from 8 career paths, dial in your salary, loan balance, and savings with sliders, and toggle real-life flags like freelancing, homeownership, and having children. PSLF eligibility is detected automatically based on your career.

Once you confirm your character, you can **record your own voice**. The browser captures 15–60 seconds of you reading the LAUNCH script, sends the audio to ElevenLabs Instant Voice Cloning, and gets back a voice ID — all without leaving the app. Voice settings (stability 0.38, similarity 0.85, speaker boost) are applied automatically via the API. From that point on, every narration in your game is read in your own cloned voice.

Custom characters skip the hard-coded anchor decisions entirely. All 12 years are generated live by Gemini, personalized to your exact financial profile.

### Analytics for Security Benefit
Every decision is stored in MongoDB — anonymized, tagged by scenario type, character, age, and optimality. After 500 players, you have a dataset that shows exactly which financial decisions graduates get wrong most, and when.

## Getting Started

### 1. Clone and install

```bash
git clone https://github.com/YOUR_ORG/launch.git
cd launch
npm install
```

### 2. Set up environment variables

Create a `.env.local` file in the root (never commit this):

```bash
# MongoDB
MONGODB_URI=mongodb+srv://username:password@cluster.mongodb.net/launch

# Gemini
GEMINI_API_KEY=your_gemini_api_key

# Auth0
AUTH0_SECRET=your_32_char_secret
AUTH0_BASE_URL=http://localhost:3000
AUTH0_ISSUER_BASE_URL=https://your-tenant.auth0.com
AUTH0_CLIENT_ID=your_auth0_client_id
AUTH0_CLIENT_SECRET=your_auth0_client_secret

# ElevenLabs (Creator plan or above required for voice cloning)
ELEVENLABS_API_KEY=your_elevenlabs_key
ELEVENLABS_VOICE_ID=your_default_voice_id  # fallback for preset characters
```

### 3. Run the development server

```bash
npm run dev
```

Open [http://localhost:3000](http://localhost:3000).

## MongoDB Collections

**`sessions`** — Full game state per player, updated after every decision.

**`analytics`** — One document per decision. Anonymized. Answers: "Which financial mistakes do graduates make most, at what age, in what scenario type?"

**`leaderboard`** — One document per completed game, sorted by final net worth.
